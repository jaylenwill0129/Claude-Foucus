## 2. Shipping Policy

**Processing time.** Orders are processed within **1–3 business days** after
purchase. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking once your order ships.

**Delivery time.** Estimated delivery is **7–12 business days** after processing for
standard shipping (some items sourced internationally may take longer; the estimate
is shown at checkout). These are estimates, not guarantees, and may vary with
carrier and customs delays.

- **Shipping cost:** Calculated at checkout (or free shipping where noted).
- **Tracking:** A tracking link is emailed when your order ships. Allow 2–4 days for
  tracking to update after dispatch.
- **Address accuracy:** Please double-check your shipping address; we are not
  responsible for orders sent to an incorrectly entered address.
- **Lost/stuck packages:** If your tracking shows no movement for 10+ business days,
  email **jaylenwill0129@gmail.com** and we'll investigate or reship.

We do not promise 1–2 day delivery; honest windows mean fewer surprises.
